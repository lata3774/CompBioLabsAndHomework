Peanut Butter Cookies
By Elise Bauer
retrieved from https://www.simplyrecipes.com/recipes/peanut_butter_cookies/ on 1/22/2020

Ingredients
	1/2 cup butter (room temperature)
	1/2 cup white granulated sugar
	1/2 cup packed brown sugar
	1/2 cup peanut butter
	1 large egg
	1 1/4 cups all purpose flour
	3/4 teaspoon baking soda
	1/2 teaspoon baking powder
	1/4 teaspoon salt
Recipe
	1. Make cookie dough
		beat butter until creamy, 2 minutes
		add brown sugar and white sugar, beat 2 minutes
		mix in peanut butter and egg
		separate bowl: whisk dry ingredients (flour, baking soda, baking powder, salt)
		stir dry ingredients into butter
	2. Chill dough 3 hours minimum
	3. Preheat oven 375 deg F
	4. shape cookies:
		1 1/4 inch balls
		3 inches apart
		flatten with fork
	5. Bake until light brown, 9 to 10 minutes, let cool on sheet for 1 minute

Option for cookie shape: Don't flatten with fork, instead press chocolate (ie. hershey kiss) into cookie right out of oven
